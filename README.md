## 🛠️ How to Use the App

Launch the application using the new shortcut on your desktop. Once the app loads, it detects your browser session. Navigate to YouTube in your preferred browser. Click the application icon to enable the floating view. The video player detaches from the webpage. It hovers over your screen. 

Adjust the size of the window by dragging the corners. Move the window anywhere on your screen. The video stays on top of your work. You can type in documents or browse other websites while the video continues to play. 

## ⌨️ Useful Keyboard Shortcuts

- Control + P: Toggle the floating window mode.
- Control + M: Mute or unmute the audio quickly.
- Control + S: Save the current video frame as an image.
- Control + Q: Close the floating window entirely.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If the window does not appear, verify that your browser is currently on a YouTube page. Ensure you have the latest version of the software installed. Check your browser plugins to ensure no conflicts exist. 

If the video freezes, restart the floating window. Use the Control + Q shortcut to close the viewer and reopen it. If the window hides behind another program, click the app icon in your taskbar to pull it to the front again. 

Should you encounter issues with audio, check the volume settings inside the floating window itself. Sometimes the mute switch inside the player syncs with your browser settings. Toggle the sound button directly on the YouTube video player to fix this synchronization.
